Raccoon Rescue

You play as a momma raccoon separated from her children in a plane crash. Your quest to reunite with your family takes you to three islands, each with its own unique challenges.

This was my first solo project in Unity. I programmed, designed, and wrote everything. I created 3 unique puzzles, a dialogue system, an attack mechanic, and all of the animations myself. It may not be perfect, but it will always be my first game.

Cephalopod Sidestep

You play an innocent octopus captured by bears. As you attempt to escape, the bear's true intentions are revealed, and it's worse than you ever could've imagined!

I, alongside my partner, developed this game over one semester for a class in the Spring of 2023. All of the mechanics, every level design, the UI, and some animations are my own work. My partner produced almost all of the art and some animations, all of which I implemented. We also worked closely with a group of students from the Berklee School of Music to produce the game's soundtrack.
